Skills Used

Cinema 4D and 3D modeling, Cinema 4D is a powerful tool for creating 3D models of objects, characters, and environments. I learned how to use various tools and techniques to create complex 3D models that can be used in animation, video games, and visual effects

Animation: Cinema 4D allowed me to animate 3D models and create dynamic motion graphics. I learned how to use keyframe animation, dynamics, and particle systems to bring my creations to life.

Adobe After Effects was used to piece it all together in a smooth visual style using different visual animations and effects to help the viewers understand.
