I leaned into a retro-futuristic aesthetic for these designs, blending space themes with adventure. "Space Coaster" came to life with halftone textures and muted blues, evoking a cosmic journey. I enjoyed layering astronaut imagery with roller coaster tracks for a surreal sense of scale and excitement. Bold, futuristic typography captured the theme's energy while blending with the spacey backdrop. My goal was to make the design immersive, balancing nostalgia with the unknown, pulling the viewer into the thrill of the ride.

Skills Learned

I learned how to layout my assets in a clean, visually appealing way and edit them together cohesively using a variety of tools in the adobe suite. Additionally following and practicing many techniques from online tutorials.

I learned how to edit my work in a non-destructive way and share the workload between programs effectively, using Illustrator for designing visual assets and Photoshop for refining details, applying textures, and finalizing the composition.

Typography was also a huge learning curve that this project helped me develop in, front contrasting fonts to using the grid system, to layouting type and using it as the posters head.
