Motion Design Project

Ravenous Fox's Cooking Video

For this Motion Design project I wanted to help out a local independent cooking business named "RavenousFox". For this I created a short, snappy, family friendly informative video that would instruct viewers to follow one of the companies recipes step by step. During the process I sketched, styleframed and planned out the whole creative process then produced it using Adobe Photoshop and After Effects, and these were my results.

I decided this project needed to have a warm, hearty feeling to it, so in my research I would find an appropriate colour scheme with relaxing, but food related colours. Then I thought about which style to choose and based on the logo and other brand materials, a fun animated cartoon design was the way to go. I then spent time researching all the cooking related video content I could find to study the forms and conventions.

The Video

Skills Used

I learned the planning process for this content form, and how to visualize and wireframe different stages of a motion graphic to maximise visual impact.

Using tools in Adobe Photoshop to help draw an accurate storyboard of events, creating all the visual assets, using colour theory, and a plethora of editing effects.

Adobe After Effects was used to piece it all together in a smooth visual style using different visual animations and effects to help the viewers understand.
