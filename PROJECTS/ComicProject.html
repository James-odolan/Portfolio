Mental Health Comic

Short Story "Joe & James"

During my final year of university one of the projects I did was a short story module, in which we had an open brief to create a short narrative that showed significant deeper meaning. My choice was to a mental health comic style page, which depicted two men in their young twenties talking about their personal feelings over a beer.

Skills Used

Photography - The photos were captured using a DSLR camera, ensuring high resolution and sharp details. Afterward, I processed the images in Adobe Lightroom, enhancing them with custom filters and color grading to achieve a stylized, less realistic color palette that aligns with the artistic vision of the project.

Adobe Photoshop - I utilized various tools within Adobe Photoshop, including the cut-out, blending, and layer adjustment features, to meticulously transform the original images. This process involved working layer by layer to morph the photos from realistic depictions into a vibrant, comic book-style narrative, bringing the story to life with a dynamic visual impact.

Graphic Design - To further enhance the comic book effect, I incorporated graphic design elements such as speech bubbles, text overlays, and custom backgrounds. These additions were carefully designed and integrated to maintain consistency with the overall theme, adding depth and narrative clarity to the final visuals.
