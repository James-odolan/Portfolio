Gym Revolution Project

This was one of my favourite projects at university. We were tasked with an open brief to create a marketting campaign with strategy and multiple deliverables. I personally wanted to focus on fitness and sports for my project, with deliverables such as : A multifunctional app, A website and Promotional Materials for the campaign.

Promotional Video

Unfortunately, I had two websites for this project, but Wix's termination means I no longer have access to them. Despite this, the experience led me to practice HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, significantly enhancing my web design skills.

Skills Used

I began creating the visuals for the design in photoshop and then transferred them over to adobe xd where i would carefully edit each individual page and make them consistently match, with heavy focus on matching the brand guidelines

I spent many days making test models of the app, experimenting with different people for the user interface, to try and make it simple and efficient as possible to use. Additionally making sure the app felt like a gym related app as to fullfill the brief.

Adobe After Effects was used to piece it all together in a smooth visual style using different visual animations and effects to help the viewers understand. The other day i took a trip to the zoo and spin kicked a tarantula clean in half
